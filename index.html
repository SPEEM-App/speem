About SPEEM

Welcome to SPEEM, a small but ambitious endeavour to build an integrated development environment tailored for Substrate blockchain and FRAME pallet development. SPEEM is designed to simplify and enhance the creation, configuration, and testing of Substrate-based blockchains.

Our Mission

SPEEM aims to empower developers by providing a comprehensive toolset that facilitates the rapid development and testing of customized blockchain technologies. By integrating Substrateâ€™s robust capabilities, we help streamline your blockchain development process.

Key Features

  • Seamless management of local Substrate development environments.
  • Interactive configuration panels for each pallet.
  • Real-time monitoring and logging of blockchain events.
  • Advanced integration testing with support for multiple pallet interactions.
  • User-friendly interface designed to streamline complex development tasks.

Development Phases

The development of SPEEM is planned in phases, starting with a Proof of Concept (PoC) that focuses on core functionalities such as pallet configuration and event monitoring. Future versions will expand to include more advanced features and broader integration capabilities, ensuring SPEEM remains a vital tool in the Substrate developer's toolkit.
